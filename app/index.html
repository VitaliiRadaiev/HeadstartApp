Every year the world produces over 275 million tonnes of plastic waste. That’s the same weight as 55 million elephants. Now more than ever, it’s important to recycle plastic and use recycled plastics where possible.

At Headstart we have developed the worlds softest plush using 100% household plastic waste. We think it’s going to change the game.
